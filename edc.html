Entrepreneurship Development Cell

Empowering students to transform innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Our Mission

To foster the entrepreneurial spirit among students by providing the necessary resources, mentorship, and support to transform innovative ideas into successful business ventures.

Objectives

  • Instill entrepreneurial mindset among students.
  • Provide training and mentorship for startup development.
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and events to promote entrepreneurship.
  • Facilitate networking opportunities with industry experts and successful entrepreneurs.
  • Offer resources and support for prototype development and business planning.

Our Activities

We conduct a variety of programs and events to nurture entrepreneurship, including:

  • Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps
  • Skill Development Workshops
  • Startup Pitching Sessions
  • Mentorship Programs
  • Networking Events with Industry Leaders
