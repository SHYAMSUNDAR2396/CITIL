INCUBATION PROGRAM

Stages of Incubation

1. Application & Selection

Startups apply for the incubator program, with selection based on innovation, feasibility, and scalability.

2. Onboarding & Orientation

Startups are introduced to facilities, mentors, and support networks available in the incubation space.

3. Market Research & Validation

Market research support is provided to validate startup ideas and optimize business models.

4. Product Development & Prototyping

Access to labs, resources, and mentorship for product development and prototyping.

5. Funding & Investment

Guidance on financial planning, fundraising, and investor connections.
